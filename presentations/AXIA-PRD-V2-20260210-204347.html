    • 1. Purpose & Vision

    1.1 Product Vision

    AXIA is a human-centric AI-powered insider risk platform designed to help security, legal, and leadership teams understand, investigate, and resolve risk events involving people and sensitive data.

    AXIA does not replace enforcement systems (DLP, email gateways, CASB). AXIA observes, explains, contextualizes, and guides human action.

    Lead Tagline: "The CISO's Front-Line AI for Insider Risk"
    Supporting Taglines:
    - "Context. Engagement. Resolution." — captures the three core pillars
    - "WHO sent it. WHAT it contains. WHO received it." — the differentiating framework

    1.2 The Problem AXIA Solves

    MetricIndustry Reality
    Average DLP alerts per day1,000+
    False positive rate70-90%
    Time to investigate one alert15-45 minutes
    Analyst burnout rate53% report burnout
    Average time to contain insider incident86 days
    Average cost per insider incident$16.2M

    Legacy DLP tools are pattern-blind (see keywords, not intent), context-deaf (treat all users the same), action-limited (block or alert only), and deployment-heavy (6-12 months to tune).

    1.3 AXIA's Answer

    AXIA is the judgment and engagement layer that:

  • Connects to existing tools (no rip-and-replace)
  • Understands context DLP misses (role, behavior, relationships, intent)
  • Judges true risk vs. noise (targeting <10% false positive rate after learning period)
  • Recommends actions (engage, escalate, block)
  • Learns continuously, making DLP smarter over time

    • The WHO / WHAT / WHO Framework

    AXIA's core analytical framework is built on three questions:

    WHO sent it. WHAT it contains. WHO received it.
    That's how you find the right cases and assign the right severity.

    Traditional DLPAXIA's Judgment
    Pattern matching on content onlySemantic understanding of content
    No understanding of sender contextRole, department, baseline behavior
    No understanding of recipient relationshipRecipient trust level and history
    Binary: block or allowContextual severity with explanation

    This framework is the foundation of AXIA's differentiation: while DLP tools focus only on WHAT the data is, AXIA understands WHY it matters based on WHO is sending it and WHO is receiving it.

    P2Multiple interactions per userAutomated sending (configurable)+ Insights, + Posture
    P3Cross-organization patternsOrchestration (email threads, auto-close)+ Enforce (smart blocks)

    Figure: Release Phase Timeline — Three-phase delivery cadence showing screen scope, AI capability progression, and case handling automation evolution.

    4.2 P1 Scope — Single Interaction, AI-Recommended Actions

    P1 delivers a complete investigation platform for single-interaction analysis with AI-powered recommendations that require human approval.

    In Scope:

    CapabilityDescription
    Command tabCase investigation workbench with severity cards, filters, case list
    Cases / Case DetailsFull investigation with structured analysis, manual actions
    Intel tabConversational AI advisor, limited to single-interaction context
    Connect tabData source management and connection health
    Settings tabConfiguration, AI boundaries, org guidance; automation toggles visible but greyed out with "Available in P2" label
    AI EngineFull structured analysis per email (What Happened / Why It Matters / Why It Is Unusual) — no behavioral baseline cross-referencing
    AI-Generated Engagement RecommendationsFor each case, AI recommends next steps with draft communication language based on criticality, reasoning, and allowed actions from Settings. All five engagement types (Employee Coaching, Manager Notification, Legal Escalation, HR Notification, Evidence Preservation) get AI-generated drafts. Operator must manually review, edit, and approve before any action executes.
    Manual case actionsAssign, close, acknowledge, escalate — all analyst-initiated after reviewing AI recommendations

    Out of Scope for P1:

    CapabilityPhaseRationale
    Insights tab (analytics dashboard)P2Requires accumulated data for meaningful trends
    Enforce tab (DLP integration view, smart block policies)P3Requires organizational intelligence + enforcement loop
    Posture tab (risk posture overview)P2Requires trend data and resolved-case history
    Automated engagement executionP2System sending emails without operator approval requires trust built during P1
    Behavioral baseline comparisonP2Requires multi-interaction data accumulation
    Cross-user / cross-org pattern detectionP3Requires organizational-level data corpus

    AI in P1:

    • Analyzes each email independently using LLM semantic analysis
    • Generates full What / Why It Matters / Why It Is Unusual structure per interaction
    • "Why It Is Unusual" derives context from role heuristics, recipient novelty, timing anomalies, and volume indicators from directory/HR data — NOT from historical behavioral baselines (which are P2)
    • Generates recommended next steps and draft engagement language (coaching email, manager briefing, legal escalation, HR notification, evidence preservation) — all require manual operator approval
    • Risk scoring based on content semantics + sender role context + recipient context (from directory/HR data), but NOT from historical behavioral patterns
    • Intel queries limited to individual case context (no trend analysis, no "show me all cases for this user")

    4.3 P2 Scope — User Behavioral Analysis, Automated Engagement

    P2 adds behavioral intelligence and engagement automation. Requires P1 deployment with accumulated interaction data.

    Adds to P1:

    CapabilityDescription
    Insights tabCurated analytics dashboard with fixed-layout widgets telling the AXIA value story
    Posture tabRisk posture score, severity distribution, trend comparison
    Behavioral baselinesAI processes multiple interactions per user to build individual and role-based behavioral baselines
    Automated engagementAutomation toggles in Settings become active. System can auto-send engagement communications based on configured severity rules. No longer requires manual approval for configured scenarios.
    Enhanced IntelUser-level analysis ("show me this person's risk history"), trend queries, cross-case pattern detection per user
    Enhanced risk scoringRisk scoring now includes behavioral deviation from individual and role baselines
    "Why It Is Unusual" upgradeNow includes historical behavioral deviation context alongside P1's role/directory heuristics

    4.4 P3 Scope — Organizational Intelligence, Orchestration

    P3 adds cross-organizational intelligence and full case orchestration. Requires mature behavioral baselines from P2.

    Adds to P2:

    CapabilityDescription
    Enforce tabDLP integration view with smart block policies. Resolved cases inform DLP policy adjustments.
    Cross-org pattern detectionAI analyzes patterns across the organization (department-level, cross-team risk concentrations)
    Case orchestrationSystem manages ongoing email conversations, auto-follows-up on unresolved coaching outreach, auto-closes cases when employee confirms compliance
    Enforcement loopContinuous learning: resolved cases → enforcement policy recommendations → DLP policy adjustments
    Enhanced IntelOrg-level analysis ("which departments are trending high-risk?"), policy effectiveness queries

    4.6 Phase Transition: P1 → P2

    When P2 ships for existing P1 customers:

    • Insights and Posture tabs appear in navigation
    • Settings automation toggles become active (previously greyed out)

    17. Engagement & Response System

    17.1 Engagement Philosophy

    AXIA's engagement system is a key differentiator. Unlike DLP (which blocks) or IRM (which alerts), AXIA orchestrates conversations and tracks resolution.

    The engagement system evolves across phases:

    • P1: AI generates all engagement drafts and recommendations. Operator reviews, edits, and manually approves before any communication is sent.
    • P2: Configurable automation. System can auto-send for configured severity levels based on Settings toggles. Legal/Critical escalations always require human confirmation.
    • P3: Full orchestration. System manages email threads, follows up on unresolved outreach, and auto-closes cases based on response patterns.
