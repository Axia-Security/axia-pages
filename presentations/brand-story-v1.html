Brand Principles

Five principles. Each drives a visual and behavioral consequence.

Vigilant, Not Anxious

Dark, calm interfaces. Subtle glows, not alarm-red flashes. We watch with purpose, not panic.

Judgment Over Enforcement

We do not just detect. We decide. Every visual element suggests analysis and intent, not walls and barriers.

Human-Centered AI

Technology that coaches rather than blocks. Warm, approachable design that partners with humans, not replaces them.

Clarity in Complexity

Clean hierarchy, clear signals, actionable insights. The complexity is in the AI. The simplicity is yours.

Trusted Advisor Presence

Confident but not arrogant. Direct but not aggressive. We are the advisor CISOs turn to, not another vendor.
