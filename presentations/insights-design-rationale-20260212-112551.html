Security Dashboards Are Broken

Three failure modes every legacy tool falls into

🚨

Alert Dumps

Legacy tools show raw alert counts. Nobody reads them. A wall of numbers creates the illusion of visibility while delivering none.

📉

No Value Story

CISOs can't answer "Is our tool working?" from a dashboard. Data exists. Meaning doesn't.

📊

Data Without Meaning

Charts exist because charts are expected, not because they answer questions. Decoration masquerading as insight.

Our Insights tab answers ONE question: "Is AXIA working?"

Three Design Principles

The rules behind every layout and widget decision

01

Story Over Data

Every widget is a chapter in the narrative "AXIA is delivering value." No decorative charts. If a widget doesn't advance the story, it doesn't belong.

Narrative-driven

02

Prominence = Importance

Widget size communicates priority. The 9-column hero (Overall Posture) is the headline. 3-column details are supporting evidence. Visual hierarchy IS information hierarchy.

Size as signal

03

Time as Narrative

The time selector doesn't just filter. It changes the story. 1d = "today's performance." 90d = "the AXIA transformation." The 90-day view is a board presentation in a single tab.

Time = perspective

Widget 1: Overall Posture

The Headline — Answers: "Are we getting better?"

KPI

RCS

Risk Control Score

Composite score (0-100). Weighted: Cases Closed Rate 40%, FP Reduction 30%, MTTR 30%.

One number the CISO takes to the board.

KPI

MTTR

Mean Time to Resolution

From 48 hours to 0.5 hours. The transformation metric.

Dual-line chart, dual Y-axes. Two KPIs, one glance.

Brand alignment: Maps to Message 6: Posture Transformation — "Risk Control Score improves from ~58 to ~94 within 90 days."

Widget 2: Cases Timeline

The Learning Proof — Answers: "Is case volume declining?"

01

Status/Severity Toggle

Same data, two perspectives, no extra widget. Status view for operations. Severity view for risk assessment.

02

Interactions Overlay

The cyan line shows AXIA analyzing MORE interactions while producing FEWER cases. This is the visual proof that AXIA is learning.

03

Declining Bars

The visual story IS the value proposition. Bars going down = AXIA working. No annotation needed. The shape tells the story.
