January 2026. Five engineers. An empty AWS account. A vision for something the security industry hadn't seen before: an AI that doesn't just detect insider risk, but understands it.


They started with the hardest part: the infrastructure. Ingestion pipelines, analysis engines, LLM services, object storage. Terraform from day one. No shortcuts.
