1. What AXIA Is

AXIA is an AI-powered Insider Risk Management (IRM) and Data Loss Prevention (DLP) intelligence platform. Positioned as "The CISO's Front-Line AI for Insider Risk" â€” a judgment and engagement layer that:

  • Observes communications across enterprise channels (email, chat, cloud storage)
  • Explains why something is risky using LLM-powered semantic analysis
  • Contextualizes risk based on role, department, behavioral baselines
  • Orchestrates human response (employee coaching, manager notification, legal escalation)
Core Philosophy: AXIA evaluates risk in context. It does NOT replace DLP enforcement â€” it complements it by understanding why something is risky and guiding what to do about it.
